GDSC

is a community of students from different colleges and universities who came together to build a globally established club to enhance the overall development of individuals and help them to excel in their careers. In GDSC, students get the opportunity to learn, explore and have hands-on experience of different types of domains through Various Events, seminars, and workshops conducted under the guidance of Google. GDSC hence becomes one of the best platforms for students to enhance their knowledge and be the best version of themselves.

About Us

Google Developer Student Clubs (GDSC) at PRMITR, Badnera is a vibrant student community aimed at fostering a passion for technology and learning among students. Our goal is to create a collaborative environment where developers, designers, and managers come together to inspire and empower thousands of individuals.
